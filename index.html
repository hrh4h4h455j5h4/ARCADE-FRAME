ABOUT ARCADE STUDIO

Arcade Studio is the elite high-performance platform for digital creators. Features animated headers, 31 custom themes (including 10 Live modes), and precision alignment tools.

OPERATIONAL GUIDELINES

1. Upload: Choose photo and frame using Steps 1 & 2.

2. Visual Style: Open the theme menu and pick one of the 10 new Live animations.

3. Symmetry: Use Center Subject for perfect alignment.

4. Lock Frame: Align Circle Size and Zoom until gaps are hidden.

5. Export: Click Download to play the success tune and save.
